## About Eterna
Eterna is on a mission to build a best-in-class, low-latency trading interface for crypto markets, but with next-gen performance. Founded by alumni of MIT, Harvard, Yale and backed by investors like Naval Ravikant and Accomplice VC.  
Our lean team is obsessed with speed, scalability, and reliability. If you dream of shipping critical infrastructure daily and want to learn firsthand how a deep-tech startup scales, this is your opportunity.
Our product, HyperFlash is a high-speed trading infrastructure for digital assets. We combine liquid staking, MEV optimization, and a low-latency trading terminal, bringing the performance of traditional finance to crypto. HyperFlash helps traders earn more, move faster, and access on-chain markets with institutional-grade precision.
